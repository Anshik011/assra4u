Pathway to Independence

Why Empowerment

True empowerment begins with self-reliance. For women and youth from marginalized backgrounds, lack of education and market-relevant skills often locks them in a cycle of economic dependency. By providing access to vocational education, training, and financial skills, we enable them to build sustainable livelihoods.

At ASSRA, we believe in long-term developmental change. Strengthening individuals to become earners doesn't just improve their own quality of life; it uplifts their entire family and local community, breaking generational chains of poverty.

Focusing on skill training and financial literacy ensures that the community grows stronger, more confident, and ready to face economic shifts independently.

Livelihood & Skill Development

What We Do

ASSRA runs intensive 'Earn while Learn' vocational projects providing sustainable livelihood training in mushroom cultivation, poultry farming (5 active farms established), sustainable agriculture on leased land, tailoring, jute, and bamboo crafts.

In urban clusters like New Delhi, ASSRA's IT Project disseminates computer basics, hardware, and software training to help needy youth secure employment in the IT sector.

We also run a unique mobile library program on cycles to engage local youth and promote reading habits in rural panchayats, and link our artisans to national markets via ARUNIM exhibitions.

Our Pillars of Work

4-Pronged Approach

Agricultural Livelihoods

Providing rural youth and landless farmers with training and resources in high-yield mushroom cultivation, poultry farming, and year-round vegetable farming on leased land.

  • Mushroom Cultivation Training
  • Poultry Farming Handholding
  • Potato & Turmeric Crop Cultivation
  • Leased Land Access for Landless
  • Commercial Agriculture Techniques

Vocational & Handicrafts

Providing hands-on vocational courses in tailoring, bamboo craft, and jute weaving to local women, fostering home-based entrepreneurship.

  • Gobarghushi Tailoring Centre
  • Decorative Bamboo Craft Making
  • Jute Curtains, Carpets & Rugs
  • Incense Stick & Candle Making
  • Micro-venture Establishment Support

IT & Hardware Training

Disseminating computer basics, hardware maintenance, and software training to aspiring and needy youth in Delhi searching for IT sector jobs.

  • Computer Hardware & Software Basics
  • Information Technology Dissemination
  • Hired Infrastructure for Practice
  • IT Career Mentoring
  • Delhi Employment Linkages

Market Access (ARUNIM)

Linking trainees directly with the market through partnerships with ARUNIM and corporate CSR week exhibitions (Coca Cola, Deloitte, etc.).

  • ARUNIM National Marketing Platform
  • CSR Week Exhibition Stalls
  • Trade Fair Representations
  • Applique Work and Spice Sales
  • Direct Sustainable Income Boost
