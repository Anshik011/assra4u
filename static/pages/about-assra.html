Who We Are

Welfare for the Vulnerable

ASSRA (Association for Social Service and Rehabilitation of the Aged) is a non-profit organisation committed to improving the quality of life of vulnerable and underprivileged communities across India.

Founded on the belief that every individual deserves dignity, security, and opportunity, ASSRA works at the grassroots level to support the elderly, underprivileged children, women, and marginalised communities.

Unlike agencies that focus solely on temporary relief, ASSRA focuses on long-term, sustainable development. Through community-based initiatives, we address social, educational, economic, and environmental challenges in close coordination with local communities and volunteers.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive and compassionate society where elderly individuals live with dignity, children have access to education, and communities are empowered to lead secure and meaningful lives.

Our Mission

  • To provide care, support, and rehabilitation services for senior citizens.
  • To promote access to education for underprivileged children and youth.
  • To empower women and communities through skill development and livelihood initiatives.
  • To encourage environmental awareness and responsible community practices.
  • To carry out all activities with integrity, transparency, and community participation.

Our Core Philosophy

ASSRA was born out of a necessity to bridge the gap between temporary aid and long-term empowerment. We recognise that while charity provides momentary relief, true community transformation comes from equipping individuals with the tools they need to succeed and thrive independently.

Whether it is ensuring a senior citizen receives medical care with dignity, providing a rural child with consistent academic support to prevent dropout, or empowering a woman with vocational skills for sustainable income, our approach is deeply holistic. We work with the community, not just for them, ensuring that our programmes—across Education, Empowerment, Elderly Care, and Environment—are culturally relevant, ethically executed, and socially accepted.

Governance

Transparency & Accountability

Trust is the foundation of our work at ASSRA. We are committed to carrying out all activities with utmost integrity and ensuring that every resource is used effectively to create meaningful impact.

ASSRA maintains proper documentation of its programmes, initiatives, and financial records. All our operations are conducted in strict compliance with applicable government regulations, ensuring full accountability. We welcome our donors, partners, and volunteers to visit our active field centres and witness the real-world impact of their contributions firsthand.
