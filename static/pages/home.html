Who We Are

Welfare for the Vulnerable

ASSRA (Association for Social Service and Rehabilitation of the Aged) is a registered non-profit organisation committed to improving the quality of life of vulnerable and underprivileged communities across India.

Founded on the belief that every individual deserves dignity, security, and opportunity, ASSRA works at the grassroots level to support the elderly, underprivileged children, women, and marginalised communities. Unlike agencies that focus solely on temporary relief, ASSRA focuses on long-term, sustainable development.

How We Work

A Structured Framework to Maximise Impact

ASSRA addresses social, educational, economic, and environmental challenges in close coordination with local communities.
SVP Model

Social Venture Philanthropy

Under the Social Venture Philanthropy (SVP) model, ASSRA identifies and implements its development projects through credible grassroots community organisations. We handhold, build operational capacities, and focus on achieving long-term sustainability, scalable workflows, and complete transparency in resources.

Direct Outreach

Direct Outreach Model

For interventions in remote rural areas, tribal hamlets, and marginalized urban pockets where local community structures do not exist, ASSRA executes projects directly. Our dedicated team of professionals and field workers manages resources directly, ensuring high social return on investment (SROI).
