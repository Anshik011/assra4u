Who We Are

About ASSRA

ASSRA (Association for Social Service and Rehabilitation of the Aged) is a non-profit organisation committed to improving the quality of life of vulnerable and underprivileged communities across India.

Founded on the belief that every individual deserves dignity, security, and opportunity, ASSRA works at the grassroots level to support the elderly, underprivileged children, women, and marginalised communities. We focus on long-term, sustainable development rather than short-term relief.
