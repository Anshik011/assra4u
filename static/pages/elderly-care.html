Welfare of the Aged

Why Elderly Care

With age comes vulnerability, and for thousands of senior citizens in India, the twilight years are marked by neglect, poverty, and abandonment. Many destitute elderly are left without shelter, primary healthcare, or a family support system.

At ASSRA, we believe that senior citizens deserve to live their remaining years with dignity, comfort, security, and respect. Our initiatives provide shelter, geriatric healthcare, nutritional security, and active family reintegration support.

Our dedicated team and volunteers work round the clock to ensure abandoned seniors find a loving family environment in our shelters, and the sick get regular medical attention.

What We Do

ASSRA runs comprehensive shelter homes for destitute and abandoned senior citizens. We ensure every resident receives regular medical checkups, balanced meals, emotional counseling, and opportunities for social recreation.

Our outreach programmes include organizing mobile health camps in rural areas, distribution of basic amenities, and supporting government geriatric welfare initiatives.

Additionally, we work closely with local administrations and police to trace families of lost elderly individuals and reunite them safely, restoring joy to fractured families.

Our Pillars of Work

4-Pronged Approach

Destitute Shelters

Providing safe, hygienic, and comfortable permanent housing for abandoned senior citizens, complete with food, security, and warm caregiver support, such as at our dedicated old age home Mo Ghara (View on Map/Photos).

  • Safe & Clean Living Spaces
  • 24/7 Wardens & Security
  • Regular Medical Inspections
  • Nutritious Home-Cooked Meals
  • Recreational & Common Areas

Geriatric Healthcare

Regular diagnostic checkups, access to essential medicines, specialized geriatric care, and distribution of physical aid instruments like walking sticks and spectacles.

  • Mobile Health Camps
  • Diagnostic Checkups
  • Free Essential Medicines
  • Physiotherapy Facilities
  • Wheelchairs & Visual Aids

Emotional Rehabilitation

Fostering mental well-being and happiness through group activities, counseling, celebrating festivals, and organizing short outings.

  • Psychological Counseling
  • Group Activities & Excursions
  • Festive & Birthday Celebrations
  • Intergenerational Programs
  • Spiritual Welfare Care

Family Reintegration

Locating families of lost or runaway seniors through a structured network, counseling the family members, and conducting safe reunions.

  • Tracing Family Details
  • Police & Network Liaison
  • Counseling Families
  • Safe Transition & Travel
  • Follow-up Welfare Checks
